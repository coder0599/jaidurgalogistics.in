About Us

Jai Durga Logistics – Reliable Transport Solutions You Can Trust

Jai Durga Logistics (JDL) is a leading transport and logistics company based in Raipur, Chhattisgarh. Our registered office is located at Sinha Complex (JDL), New Bilaspur Road, Ring Road No. 04, Beside Induria Petrol Pump, Tatibandh, Raipur (C.G) – 492099. The company is owned and managed by Proprietor: Mr. Kundan Sinha & Mr. Dilip sinha with a strong focus on reliability, transparency, and long-term client relationships.
We operate with a powerful fleet of more than 100 trucks in our group and provide transportation services across Raipur, Raigarh, entire Chhattisgarh, and all over India, with special strength in Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh. JDL works with many reputed industrial and private limited companies, including:
Jaiswal Neco Pvt. Ltd. Sambhav Steel Pvt. Ltd. Bajrang Power Pvt. Ltd. Goel Ispat Pvt. Ltd. Vandana Global Pvt. Ltd. Godawari Ispat Pvt. Ltd. Nakoda Ispat Pvt. Ltd. And many more leading companies We provide both-side booking facilities, ensuring smooth logistics movement: From Raipur / Chhattisgarh to Maharashtra & Gujarat From Maharashtra to Chhattisgarh From Gujarat to Chhattisgarh Our services cover all types of trucks from 10 MT to 40 MT, available at multiple locations at any time, ensuring flexibility and timely dispatch. You are just one step away from your material being delivered to your desired destination.
